Segments

One platform, configured for your kind of hospital

The platform does not fork per segment. What differs is configuration — pathways, rules, roles, forms and reporting — set up for how your hospital actually works, and changeable by you afterwards.

Multi-speciality hospitals

A patient's journey crosses six departments and four systems, and the handoffs are where time and money leak.

Medical colleges

Residents document care that a consultant is accountable for, and the supervision trail is informal.

Government hospitals

Outpatient volumes make every extra click a queue.

Eye hospitals

Clinic throughput is the business model, and every workflow second matters at volume.

Oncology

Regimens are protocol-defined and dose calculations are unforgiving.

IVF & fertility

Care is organised as cycles and attempts, not episodes — a shape most systems cannot express.

Mother & child

Mother and baby are separate patients whose records must be permanently linked.

Dental

Findings and treatments are per-tooth and per-surface; free text destroys the data.

Diagnostic chains

Samples are collected at many points and processed centrally; custody and timing are everything.
